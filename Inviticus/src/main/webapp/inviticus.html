What is inviticus?

The goal of inviticus is to bring high-level athletes together; both
online and in the real-world. The Internet is a massive place, and there
exist web sites and forums and blogs that do a really good job of this.
Shout out to ...... The problem that we want to fix is that there are so many
posts on so many platforms...Inviticus aims to bring this spread out information to
one place.

The individual athlete and individual doctors are what knows .....Make the connection
between people being the containers of information. The best way to learn something is
for someone to teach you in person. Inviticus wants these athletes to come together and
teach each other their ways to the top.
